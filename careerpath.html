Faculty of Business And Economics

The Faculty of Business and Economics prides itself in its mandate to prepare skilled graduates to participate successfully in the economic and business sectors of South Africa and beyond. it offer a broad selection of business qualifications, from Paralegal, Hospitality, Tourism Sport, business, sociology, statistics, Retail, Marketing and others.

Faculty of Engineering, Built Environment & IT

As South Africa prepares for the Fourth Industrial Revolution (4IR), the Faculty of Engineering, Built Environment and Information Technology(EBIT), focuses its research, teaching and learning activities on bringing competitive knowledge to industry, education, and society. EBIT is highly ranked globally; programmes are accredited by statutory and professional bodies at both national and international levels. the aim for research excellence and are keen on international collaboration. The Faculty is highly organized.

Faculty of ART , Design and Architecture

School of the Arts strives to be the academic locus in South Africa that produces the most distinguished postgraduate researchers, prolific creative talent and performing artists, and provide access and opportunity across demographics to all talented students from all South African communities who wish to maintain standards of excellence and thereby make a contribution.

Faculty of humanities

The Faculty of humanities has a long and proud tradition of excellence in the education of humanities. Driven by cutting edge research in the creation of teaching and learning in line with the fourth industrial revolution (4IR) in order to graduate a new generation of humanities professionals. Students who join the Faculty receive a rich and varied hands-on clinical experience in a range of humanities, and community settings thus becoming a productive part of society and communities with long term positive impact. Students are trained for marketing, HR, Public relations and others.
